Trend Overview
Highlights
  • The average life expectancy at birth in 2024 is approximately 75.2 years, with females at ~77.8 years and males at ~73.0 years.
  • Chinese Malaysians continue to register the highest life expectancy among major ethnic groups.
  • Petaling, Selangor stands out with one of the highest district-level life expectancies at 80.2 years.

Did You Know?

🧠 Malaysian females live 4–6 years longer than males on average, across all ethnic groups.
